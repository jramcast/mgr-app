Music Genre Classification/Recognition with Audioset

This is a demo of Music Genre Classification/Recognition as developed in the study Machine learning for music genre: multifaceted review and experimentation with Audioset . This demo includes four different estimators models, trained with the VGGish Audioset features, and using the audio of any Youtube video as input.

Classification is performed on 10-second segments as the video clip plays in the background. Each graph shows the top-10 genres for a clip as a result of taking those with the highest average score for all clip segments.
