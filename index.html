Music Genre Classification with Audioset

Demo of Music Genre Classification/Recognition(MGC/MGR) using artificial intelligence as developed in the study ANONYMIZED FOR REVIEW .

The demo includes four different estimators models, trained with the VGGish Audioset features, and accepts any Youtube video as input. Classification is performed on 10-second segments as the video clip plays in the background. Each graph shows the top-10 genres for a clip as a result of averaging genre scores along all clip segments, and selecting the highest 10.
